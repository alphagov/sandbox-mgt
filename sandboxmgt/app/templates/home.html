Government data science sandbox

Create your own government data science sandbox to try new ways of analysing data, improve your coding skills or to help build a case for your department to invest in data science tools.

{% endblocktrans %}
{% blocktrans %}

Signing up to the alpha

To sign up to the alpha version of this service, you need a government email address and a GitHub account. We use GitHub to authenticate you and to save the work you do in your sandbox.

{% endblocktrans %}

Your commitments

For security reasons, only open data can be loaded into your government data science sandbox. In signing up for one, you’ll be agreeing not to load any sensitive, personal or classified data. You’ll also be agreeing to uphold the Data Science Ethical Data Framework.

{% blocktrans %}

Get help

The government data science sandbox is being developed by the Better Use of Data team at the Government Digital Service.

Contact the team if you have feedback, need help, or have a question.
